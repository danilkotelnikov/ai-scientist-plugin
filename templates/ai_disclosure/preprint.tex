\section*{AI Disclosure}

This manuscript was prepared using the \textbf{Vedix research workbench}
(\VEDIXVENUE\ venue profile). The pipeline orchestrated specialized
agents through a sequence of phases. Sentence-level provenance is
recorded in \texttt{provenance.jsonl} — one entry per sentence with the
responsible agent, the LLM model that generated it, the cited evidence,
and the number of self-reflection rounds.

The human author(s) reviewed every Vedix-emitted sentence, verified
every citation, ran the reproducibility audit
(\texttt{reproducibility\_audit.json}), inspected the pre-registration
audit (\texttt{prereg\_audit.json}), and signed off on the final
manuscript. Author identities and corresponding responsibilities appear
